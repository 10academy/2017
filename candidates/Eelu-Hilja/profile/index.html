In other respects

I am a 2016 graduate of the BSc Honours in Molecular and Cell Biology at the University of Cape Town. I am also a Young African Leadership Initiative (YALI) Southern African Cohort 7 alumnus and an Allan Gray Orbis Fellow.

My ambition is to build research and science capacity in Namibia, and more importantly an institution to carry out scientific and technological research for national development. Fun stuff.
