Get to know Joylene Tinaye Dzoro

Joylene is a proud social worker who studied at Bindura University of Science Education in Zimbabwe. She is interested in research and policy analysis. She has an in-depthknowledge of the needs of vulnerable populations. She cares about other young people especially those who cannot access basic education. She wishes to make a positive impact in the lives of young people by making the environment conducive for them, so that they realise their potential.

Brief history of Joylene

Joylene was born on a Tuesday, 15 March 1994 in Gutu. She grew up in a small town, Chiredzi which is in the south of Zimbabwe. Joylene has 3 siblings, Miriro, Tinotenda and Ropafadzo. She attended her elementary school at Uncle Jack Pre-school. She did her primar education at Chiredzi Government Primary School. During the holidays, she went to her rural area, Benzi in Zaka where all her relatives are.Joylene can do all the traditional house chores which include sweeping the farm yard, fethcing water at the well, herding cattle and plastering the floor with cow dung. She went to Mashoko High school for her secondary education. There she did her Advanced level and came out with 13 points. Just after her secondary school, she facilitated a grade four class as a temporary teacher in Zaka district.

Her dreams

In her rural community, many children do not have access to primary education because there are very few schools which are located far away fom their areas. A significant number of both girls and boys drop out of school because they fail to endure the long distances. Joylene wishes to make this environment friendly for all children, by coming up with local areas where a group of children can meet and have their classes.
