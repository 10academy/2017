Joy Muli

Profile

Joy is a passionate designer from Nairobi, Kenya. She graduated with honours with a Bachelor's degree in Design from the University of Nairobi. She has previously worked for a number of companies in Kenya as a graphic designer and interior designer and was the winner of an interior design competition organised by The Centre for Biotechnology and Bioinformatics at The University of Nairobi. She was also 2nd runners up in the African Fund for Endangered Wildlife Art Competition. Joy has taken part in various design thinking workshops including the Nairobi Design Week.

While at Studio 62 Limited, Joy has displayed her leadership skills. She was the Lead Designer on various projects where her roles included design conceptualization, schematic design, facilitation of tender processes and site inspection and supervision. As a 10 Academy Fellow, Joy has gained training in Modern Skills; Design thinking, Making, 3D printing, Mathematics and Physics, Entrepreneurship and Management, Economics, Data and Society, Communications and Software Development.

Joy's technical skill set includes proficiency in Archicad, Lumion, Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Sketchup. Her soft skills include Design Thinking, Innovation, Communication, Negotiation, Creativity, Teamwork and Networking. During her free time she enjoys travelling, reading about current world development, drawing, painting and experiencing different cultures.
