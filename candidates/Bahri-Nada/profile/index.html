Who is Nada Bahri ?

Nada is a law student from TUNISIA specializing in private law at the University of Law and Political Sciences.

She had her first internship with STRATEGE an HR consulting firm where she dealt with HR and administration tasks. She also had an internship with IDEA MENA new founded branch in Tunisia.

She held the position of HR assistant at CROWN HOLDINGS.

Nada is passionate about humanitarian work, volunteerism and social network.

Nada is trained locally and internationally in 21st century soft skills like: conflict resolution, team management, map thinking, logistics, sales and communication.

Nada had her latest training with 10 Academy 2017 a project of IPC in partnership with Ashesi . The program included training in Design thinking, Coding & software, Making, 3D printing, Big Data, Entrepreneurship and management.

Nada represented Tunisia at the Future Forum “Strengthening Young People’s Roles in Peace Process” in Swedish Institute Alexandria, Egypt.
