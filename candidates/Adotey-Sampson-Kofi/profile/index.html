SAMPSON ADOTEY JNR

Communication Specialist | 10 Academy Fellow


Akwaaba!

Sampson Adotey Jnr is a Global Shaper of the World Economic Forum and a communications specialist by profession. He holds a Bachelor of Arts (Hons) degree in Integrated Development Studies (Development Communication option) from the University of Development Studies. As a former Chief of Innovations and co-founder of a startup in the agriculture sector, Sampson has contributed to improving the livelihoods of many farmers through innovative and user friendly technology. He is also a 10 Academy Fellow and a change maker.

Sampson has 5 years communications working experience. He has worked as a broadcast journalist with Ultimate 94.7 in Wa, Bishara FM in Tamale, and Joy News on MultiTv. As a student, he worked as a Public Relations Officer for the Student Representative Council at the University for Development Studies (Wa Canpus) where he was a member of several University committtees. He was also a Campus Ambassador of Ghana Student Discount Card. Also, he has worked in the Water, Sanitation and Hygiene sector as Project supervisor with Water Resources Consultancy. As a active volunteer he has been involved in several volunteering projects such as leading the Social Media team of Agribootcamp16 organized by DAIN Network with Support from GhanaThink Foundation, Guiness Ghana, Olam and World Bank Ghana Office. He is the marketing lead for Barcamp Wa, a flagship program organized yearly by GhanaThink Foundation to raise a critical and conscious mass of young people for action in Ghana.

As an ardent reader, writer and blogger, he has several features published both in a national daily and online to his credit. He is very passionate about issues on leadership and governance, climate change indigenous and international development.
