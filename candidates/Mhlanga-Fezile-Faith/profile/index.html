For the love of nature

Our environment is degrading at an almarming rate due to anthropogenic activities and it is up to this generation to mitigate the effects of humans on the environment so that the future generation can enjoy a healthy environment. My passion for nature and love for children inspired me to study a BSc in Conservation Ecology from the University of Stellenbosch. I want to conserve our ecosystems and promote the sustainable use of natural resources for the future generation to benefit.

I am particularly interested in soil conservation which is often ovelooked. Soil supports all terrestrial life on Earth and therefore is vital for the existence of mankind. Some of the food consumed by people and animals comes from plants which gets nutrients from the soil. Healthy soils also absorbs excess water which recharges the water table. They also purify water as it leaches into the ground. I hope through my work, I will get people to appreciate soil as much as I do and encourage soil conservation.
