CLASS OF 2017!

The 2017 inaugural class of 10 Academy Fellows is a diverse group of talent, united in their desire to use their skills, energies and intelligence, catalyzed by modern skills and technology, to have exceptional impact in Africa.

The class comes from all parts of Africa, 15 different countries and has 55% women.

Process Africa | Solving Africa's problems

Mulung, Bibi Rawiyah Mauritius

Rawiyah holds a BEng Electrical & Electronics Engineering degree from the University of Technology Brunei. She was awarded a merit-based scholarship by the Government of Brunei Darussalam. Among her numerous achievements, Rawiyah was the winner of the National Science Project Competition (Mauritius) in 2010, the Heman Guness Creativity Award by UNESCO (Mauritius) in 2011, and the University of Technology Brunei Public Speaking Competition in 2014. Rawiyah is also a freelance article writer and editor.
